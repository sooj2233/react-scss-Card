Electing a Pope
When a pope dies there are many things that the Vatican must do before a new pope takes office. The busiest person during this period is the camerlengo , or papal chamberlain. First he confirms the pope’s death and locks the pope’s private apartment. He removes the Ring of the Fisherman, which is a symbol of the pope’s power.
